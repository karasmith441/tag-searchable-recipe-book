Chicken Tikka Masala

#chicken #curry #indian #marinade

Equipment

  • Grill
  • Dutch Oven

Ingredients

  • 2 Lbs Chicken Thighs

Marinade

  • 2/3 Cup Yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp. Garam Masala
  • 1 Tbsp. Paprika
  • 2 tsp. Cumin
  • 2 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp. Cayenne Powder
  • 1 tsp. Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • Lime Juice and Salt

Spice Blend

  • 1 Tbsp. Garam Masala
  • 1 Tbsp. Red Curry Powder
  • 1 Tbsp. Ground Fenugreek Seeds
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • A Pinch of Nutmeg

Curry

  • 1/2 a head of Garlic
  • 1 White/Yellow Onion
  • 28 oz. can of Tomato Puree
  • A big spoonful of Ghee
  • A Handful of Curry Leaves
  • Lime Juice
  • 1/4 Cup Heavy Cream

Instructions

Marinade

  1. Combine all of the marinade ingredients and apply to the Chicken. Set in the fridge in a sealed bag for at least 2 hours.

Prep

  1. Heat a Dutch oven (or deep tomato safe skillet) on the stove. Do not add oil and place in cloves from half a head of garlic.
  2. Remove the Half Head of Garlic once browned, peel and mince it. Also mince One Onion. (I recommend roughly chopping the onion and throwing all your alliums in a food processor)
  3. Start your grill.

Cook

  1. Grill the marinaded Chicken.
  2. While it's grilling, heat A Big Spoonful of Ghee in a Dutch oven, then add the Allium mixture. Sweat until translucent.
  3. Add the spice blend and toast.
  4. Pour the Can of Tomato Puree, then add the Handful of Curry Leaves and a bit of Lime Juice.
  5. The Chicken is probably done, go check on that if you haven't. While the curry sauce is simmering, cut the chicken into bite sized pieces.
  6. Once the curry sauce has thickened, whisk in 1/4 Cup of Heavy Cream, stirring constantly to stop the cream from curdling.
  7. Add the Chicken. Simmer until the curry has re-thickened.

Notes

Serve with Rice or Naan.
