Easy Chocolate Chip Cookies

#cookie #easy #quick #treat

Equipment

  • Stand Mixer
  • Baking Sheet & Parchment Paper

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Flour
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Cup Softened Butter
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375
  2. Combine 3 Cups Flour, 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Baking Soda, 1 Tsp Baking Powder in a medium bowl and stir to combine.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat together 1 Cup each Butter, Granulated Sugar, and Brown Sugar until light.
  4. Beat in 2 Eggs and 1 Tbsp (or more) Vanilla extract.
  5. Slowly add the dry ingredients while mixing to combine.
  6. Mix in the Chocolate Chips (or whatever you want to put in the cookies).
  7. Make ~1 inch balls of dough, put on a baking sheet with parchment paper (or some other anti-sticking implement), smush down a little (should still be > 1/2 inch tall), and bake for 12 minutes, checking and rotating after 8 minutes.
  8. Let cool on the baking hot sheet for 2 minutes, then remove and let rest on a wire rack.
