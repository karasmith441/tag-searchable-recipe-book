Pesto-Walnut Chicken

#chicken #pasta

Equipment

  • A Pot
  • A Pan

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Package of Spaghetti
  • 1 Small Jar of Pesto
  • 1 Cup Walnuts
  • Ginger
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Start water boiling, then put in pasta for package specified amount of time.
  2. Meanwhile, pound chicken breasts until thin, then spice with Ginger, Paprika, Salt, and Pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the Chicken
  4. Take out Chicken and let rest.
  5. Once the Pasta is done, drain it, reserving half a cup of pasta water.
  6. Pour the Pasta and reserved water into the skillet that the Chicken was cooked in, then add your desired amount of Pesto and Walnuts.
  7. Mix well to combine and serve with sliced chicken.
