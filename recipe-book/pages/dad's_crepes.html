Dad's Crepes

Batch and Time

Takes 30 Minutes
Makes 6 Crepes

Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 1/2 cups Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tbsp Neutral Oil
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Heat an oiled/greased skillet over medium heat
  3. Pour 1/3 cup measures into a greased skillet. Rotate the skillet until the batter covers the bottom of the skillet.
  4. Once the edges of the crepe start to curl, flip the crepe and cook for another 30 seconds, then remove from heat and add your favorite toppings.

Notes

If you want a savory crepe, remove the vanilla extract. The vanilla in the batter just doesn't play nice with savory fillings.
