Adobo Sauce

#mexican #sauce #spicy #vegetarian

Equipment

  • Skillet
  • Food Processor

Ingredients

  • 4 Dried Guajillo Peppers
  • 4 Dried Ancho Peppers
  • 3 Cups Hot Water (Enough to cover dried the chilies)
  • 1 Tsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 An Onion
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Oregano
  • 1 Tsp Sugar
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Veggie Stock (see step 5)

Instructions

  1. De-stem and de-seed 4 Dried Guajillo Peppers and 4 Dried Ancho Peppers, then toast them in a dry hot pan
  2. Remove the peppers from the pan and soak in hot water for ~20 minutes until rehydrated.
  3. While the peppers are rehydrating quarter half an Onion. Then toast 4 Cloves of Garlic (skin on) and the Onion chunks, tossing occasionally, until black char spots appear.
  4. Skin the Garlic, then add it and the charred Onion to a food processor. Add 1 Tsp each Cinnamon, Cumin, Oregano, and Sugar. Once the chilies are rehydrated, add them to the food processor as well.
  5. Blitz the ingredients in the food processor, adding Veggie stock as necessary to thin the sauce to your preferred consistency.
  6. Add salt (and sugar and other spices) until it tastes good.
