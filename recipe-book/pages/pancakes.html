Pancakes

#treat

Batch and Time

Takes 20 Minutes
Makes 6-8 Pancakes

Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Griddle or Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 1 1/4 Cups Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp Melted butter
  • 1 tbsp Baking powder
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk 1.5 Cups Flour, 1 Tbsp Baking powder, and a pinch of salt together until mixed.
  2. Add 1.25 Cups Milk, 1 Egg, 1 Tbsp Vanilla, and 1 Tbsp Melted Butter; then whisk until smooth.
  3. Put 1/3 cup dollops of batter on a hot well buttered/greased griddle/skillet'.
  4. Cook pancakes until you can see a lightly browned rim on the bottom and holes on the top, then flip and cook until fluffy.
