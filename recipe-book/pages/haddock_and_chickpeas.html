Haddock and Chickpeas

#fish #haddock #pescetarian #seafood

Equipment

  • 2 Skillets
  • Shallow Dish

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 2 Haddock Filets
  • 1 Shallot
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Tsp Lemon Juice
  • A Couple Sprigs of Parsley
  • Olive Oil

Chickpeas

  • 15 Oz. Can of Chickpeas
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper
  • A Splash of White Wine

Instructions

  1. Drain the 15 oz. chickpeas and pat dry
  2. To make the marinade: finely chop 4 Cloves of Garlic, 1 Shallot, and a few sprigs of Parsley, then combine with 1 Tbsp Melted Butter, 1 Tsp Lemon Juice, and enough Olive Oil in a shallow dish to make the mixture a thin sauce.
  3. Pat dry the Haddock Filets and then place them in the marinade
  4. Heat two pans on the stove with a bit of oil, the first on medium heat, the second on medium-high.
  5. Put the Chickpeas in one pan and season with Salt, Pepper, Paprika, and Garlic powder.
  6. Place the Fish in the second pan, marinade side down, for 5-6 minutes (until the bottom is crispy).
  7. Flip the Fish. The Chickpeas should be drying out about now, so also add A Splash of White Wine and 1 Tbsp of Butter to them.
  8. Once the wine has evaporated, nestle the Fish in the Chickpeas. After about 3 minutes the Fish and Chickpeas should be done.
