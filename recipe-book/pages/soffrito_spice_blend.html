Soffrito Spice Blend

#mediterranean #soffrito #spice blend

Batch and Time

Takes 5 Minutes
Makes 3 1/3 Tbsp

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Dried Minced Onions
  • 1 Tbsp Dried Parsley
  • 2 Tsp Dried Minced Sundried Tomatoes
  • 2 Tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Rosemary
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Sage
