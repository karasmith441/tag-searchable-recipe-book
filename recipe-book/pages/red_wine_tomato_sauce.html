Red Wine Tomato Sauce

#italian #pasta sauce #red sauce #sauce #vegetarian

Equipment

  • Large Tomato Safe Pan/Pot

Ingredients

  • 28 oz. Can of Peeled Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Medium Onion
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 Cup Red Wine
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • A Big Handful of Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 1-2 Sprigs Fresh Thyme
  • 1-2 Sprigs Fresh Oregano
  • 1-2 Sprigs Fresh Rosemary
  • An Orange
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a dry pot or pan on high heat, place 4 Whole Cloves of Garlic skin on and cook until charred. While the Garlic is charring finely chop your Onion.
  2. Once the Garlic is charred remove it and turn down the heat to medium and add the Onions. Then squeeze the Garlic out of its skin onto a cutting board and finely chop. Then add back to the pot.
  3. Once the Onions and Garlic are fragrant, add 2 Tbsp of Butter, 1/2 a Cup of Red Wine, A Big Handful of Basil, and Sprigs of Thyme, Oregano, and Rosemary. Bring to a simmer and reduce on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sauce can coat the back of a spoon
  4. Add the 28 oz. Can of Tomatoes to the pot and crush them with a wooden spoon/spatula until the sauce is just a little chunky. (Alternatively use an emersion blender for a smoother sauce).
  5. Once the Tomato is crushed, squeeze a bit of orange juice in and let simmer. Occasionally taste and add herbs/salt/juice until it feels ready.
