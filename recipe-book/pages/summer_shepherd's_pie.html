Summer Shepherd's Pie

#beef #irish

Equipment

  • Dutch Oven

Ingredients

Mashed Potatoes

  • 2 Lbs Potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Sour Cream
  • Salt and Pepper

Filling

  • 1 Lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Lbs Mushrooms
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 of a Zucchini
  • 2 Tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 3/4 Cup Broth
  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Corn
  • A Pinch of Saffron
  • A Sprig each Rosemary, Thyme, and Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

Prep

  1. Mince 1 Medium onion, 6 Cloves of Garlic, 1/2 Lbs of Mushrooms.
  2. Chop 1/2 of a Zucchini into 1/4 inch pieces.
  3. Peel and cube 1 Pound of Potatoes and put in a pot with cold water.
  4. Place saffron in a small bowl of water (~2 Tbsp water.)

Cook

  1. Bring the water with the Potatoes to a boil and cook until tender.
  2. Drain the water, then add 2 Tbsp Butter, 2 Tbsp Sour Cream, Salt, and Pepper and mash.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 F
  4. In a Dutch oven, brown 1 Pound of Ground Beef.
  5. Once the beef is browned, remove it. Add onion, and sweat until fragrant, then add the Garlic.
  6. Add 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste and the Zucchini. Sear for just a bit before adding 3/4 Cups of Broth, 1 Tsp Worcestershire sauce, 1/2 Cup corn, Sprigs of Rosemary, Thyme, and Oregano, and the soaked Saffron. Make sure the liquid just covers the other ingredients (add water if you must).
  7. Bring to a boil then make a cornstarch slurry with 2 Tbsp Cornstarch.
  8. Add the cornstarch, cook until the sauce has thickened, and then remove from the heat.
  9. Spread the mashed potatoes to form a thick layer over the filling, leaving some gaps near the side for steam to escape.
  10. Cook uncovered for 20 minutes.
