PeanutScotch Cookies

#cookie #treat

Equipment

  • Stand Mixer
  • Baking Sheet & Parchment Paper
  • Shallow Bowl

Ingredients

Butterscotch

  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla

Dough

  • 1/2 Cup Peanutbutter
  • 1/2 Cup Butterscotch
  • 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 6 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tbsp Scotch
  • 1 2/3 Cups Flour
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • Finishing Sugar (Used on day 2)

Instructions

  1. Put 6 Tbsp Butter, 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar, 1/2 Cup Peanut Butter, 1/4 Cup Packed Brown Sugar, and 2 Tsp of Vanilla in the bowl of a stand mixer. Do not mix yet.

Butter Scotch

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat melt 2 Tbsp of Butter
  2. Once the Butter is melted add in 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar and stir until molten
  3. Add in 1/4 Cup Heavy cream. Boil until the sauce starts to thicken (about 4 minutes)

Dough

  1. Pour the Butterscotch into the stand mixer bowl (should be ~1/2 Cup) and mix until all ingredients are incorporated.
  2. Add the Egg, 1 Tbsp Vanilla, and 1 Tbsp Scotch. Beat until incorporated
  3. Dry mixture -> In a small bowl, add 1 1/2 Cups Flour, 1 Tsp Baking Soda, and 1 Tsp Salt.
  4. 1/2 Cup at a time add the dry mixture to the stand mixer and mix to incorporate, scraping the sides with a spatula after each mix. Once the last of the dry mixture is incorporated, continue mixing for about 3 minutes for the dough to come together.
  5. Cover the dough and let rest in the fridge overnight

The Next Day

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F
  2. Make teaspoon sized balls of dough and roll them in the Finishing Sugar then place on a non-stick baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 12 minutes then let rest on the baking sheet for 3 minutes before moving to a wire rack to finish cooling.
