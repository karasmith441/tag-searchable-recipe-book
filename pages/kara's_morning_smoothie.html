Kara's Morning Smoothie

#low ingredient #quick #smoothie #vegetarian

Batch and Time

Makes 1 smoothie
Takes 15 minutes

Equipment

  • blender

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen Banana (Split into chunks)
  • 5 Dates
  • A Spoonful of Cacao Nibs (chocolate chips are an alternative if you don't have nibs)
  • A Spoonful of Peanut Butter
  • A Handful of Spinach (Optional)
  • Enough Milk (or Milk Substitute) to make the smoothie smooth

Instructions

  1. Put ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. Add more liquid if necessary.
