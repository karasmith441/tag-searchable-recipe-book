Straw-Pom Smoothie

#low ingredient #quick #smoothie #vegetarian

Batch and Time

makes 1 smoothie
takes 15 minutes

Equipment

  • Blender

Ingredients

  • 2 Handfuls Frozen Strawberries
  • 2 Heaping Spoonfuls Honey Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • 1/2 Cup Protein Powder
  • 3 Kale Leaves (Stems Removed)
  • 1-2 Cups Pomegranate Juice

Instructions

  1. Add non-liquids into blender along with ~1 cup of Pomegranate Juice.
  2. Blend until smooth, add more Juice as necessary.
