Aloo Tikkis with Broccoli

#indian #side #snack #vegetarian

Batch and Time

makes 12 Tikkis
takes 20 minutes

Equipment

  • Pot
  • Potato Masher
  • Skillet

Ingredients

  • 2 Lbs Potatoes
  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • 3/4 Cup Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • 4 Green Onions
  • 1 Thai Green Chili
  • 1 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Chaat Masala
  • 1 Tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 Tsp Black pepper
  • Fine Salt and Large Grain Salt
  • 1 Large Egg
  • Cooking Oil

Instructions

  1. Dice 2 Lbs Potatoes into small cubes, and finely chop 1 Head of Broccoli into very small pieces.
  2. Boil Potatoes until mostly done, then add the Broccoli and continue boiling until Potatoes are done.
  3. Drain and use a potato masher to coarsely mash vegetables.
  4. Add 3/4 Cup Breadcrumbs, 1/3 Cup Chopped Parsley, 4 Finely Chopped Green Onions, 1 Minced Thai Green Chili, 1 Tsp each Cumin, Chaat Masala, Garam Masala, and Ground Black Pepper. Mix to combine and salt to taste.
  5. Add the Egg and mix to combine, then shape into small patties (should make ~12).
  6. Heat about 1/8 inch of oil in a large skillet, then fry the patties until browned on both sides, flipping after 2-3 minutes. (You may have to work in batches depending on the size of your skillet.)
  7. Sprinkle with Large Grain Salt immediately after removing from the skillet.
