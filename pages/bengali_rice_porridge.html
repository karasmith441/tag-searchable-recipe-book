Bengali Rice Porridge

#chicken #indian #rice

Equipment

  • Dutch Oven

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt & Pepper

Rice

  • 2 Tbsp Ghee
  • 1 Inch Fresh Ginger Root
  • 1 Inch Fresh Turmeric Root
  • 1 Cup Short Grain Rice
  • 1/4 Cup Red Lentils
  • 6 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1/2 Cup Sherry (or just more stock if you don't have sherry)
  • 1/2 Tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • 2 Thai Green Chilies
  • 1 Lime

Instructions

  1. Pound flat and spice the chicken breasts with paprika, garlic powder, and salt & pepper, then sear in a Dutch oven.
  2. Sliver 1 Inch Fresh Ginger Root and 1 Inch Fresh Turmeric Root . Once the chicken is cooked through, remove it and add 2 Tbsp Ghee to the Dutch oven, then fry the ginger and turmeric in the ghee.
  3. Add 1 Cup Short Grain Rice and toast, then add 1/4 Cup Red Lentils and 6 Cups Chicken Broth. Stir continuously.
  4. Once the liquid is absorbed, add 1/2 Cup of Stock and keep stirring. Repeat until rice is tender and slow to absorb liquid.
  5. Slice the chicken into 1/2 inch strips and finely chop 2 Thai Green Chilies , then add The Chicken, Green Chilies, and 1/2 Tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder .
  6. Garnish with Lime Juice and Parsley.
