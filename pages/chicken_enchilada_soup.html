Chicken Enchilada Soup

#chicken #mexican #soup

Equipment

  • Big Pot or Dutch Oven
  • Medium Bowl
  • Small Bowl

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 Lb Chicken Thighs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder

Soup

  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 A Jalapeño
  • 2 Chipotles, chipotle in Adobo
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Tbsp Yellow Onion, onion
  • 9 Cups Stock (7 for the soup, 2 for the masa.)
  • 1 15 oz Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cups Frozen Corn
  • 3/4 Cup Masa Harina
  • 4 oz Cream Cheese
  • A Generous Handful of Fresh Parsley
  • Lime Juice
  • Salt & Pepper

Spice Blend

  • 3 Tbsp Ancho Chili Powder
  • 1 Tsp Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Coriander
  • 1/2 Tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 Tsp Oregano
  • 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon

Instructions

Prep

  1. Season Chicken with salt, pepper, cumin, paprika, and garlic powder.
  2. Finely chop 1 Yellow Onion , 6 Cloves of Garlic, 1/2 A Jalapeño, and 2 Chipotles, chipotle in Adobo
  3. In a small bowl, mix the spice blend.

Cook

  1. In an oiled Dutch oven, sear Chicken until almost cooked.
  2. Remove Chicken, then put in Onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add the Spice Blend and 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste and sauté a bit longer.
  4. Add 7 Cups of Stock, Chicken Thighs, 1 Tbsp Yellow Onion, onion, 2 Cups Frozen Corn , the Can of Fire Roasted Tomatoes, the Jalapeño, and the Chipotle Peppers.
  5. In a meduim bowl, whisk 3/4 Cup Masa Harina and 2 Cups of Stock together until smooth
  6. Add Masa Harina mixture to the Dutch oven, then mostly cover and let simmer for 12-15 minutes or until chicken is tender enough to
  7. shred.
  8. Remove Chicken and shred with two forks.
  9. Add in 4 oz Cream Cheese and shredded cheese and stir until melted.
  10. Add back the chicken (again). Squeeze in Lime Juice, and Salt and Pepper to taste.
