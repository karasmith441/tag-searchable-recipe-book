Key Lime Tarts

#sweets #over an hour

Batch and Time

makes ~12 Tarts
takes ~4.5 hours

Equipment

  • 2 Bowls and a Spice Grinder or one Bowl and a Mortar an Pestle
  • Cupcake Molds

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1.5 Packs Biscoff Cookies
  • 1/3 Cup Packed Brown Sugar
  • 4 Tbsp Butter

Filling

  • 2 14 oz. Cans Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 3/4 Cup Lime Juice
  • Zest of One Lime

Instructions

Crust

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Grind to dust your 1.5 Packs of Biscoff Cookies and melt 4 Tbsp of Butter.
  3. In a bowl, mix biscoff, butter, and 1/3 Cup of Packed Brown Sugar.
  4. Pack biscoff mix into cupcake molds covering the bottom and sides, creating a bowl for the filling. (I found using a mortar helps with both the grinding and the shaping)
  5. Bake crusts for 10 minutes.

Filling

  1. In another bowl, mix both cans of Sweetened Condensed Milk, 1 Cup of Greek Yogurt, 3/4 Cup of Lime Juice, and lime zest to a large bowl. Whisk until homogenous.
  2. Spoon the filling into the still hot crust and return to the oven for 15 minutes.
  3. Let cool for 20 minutes at room temperature then move to the fridge to chill for 3 hours.
