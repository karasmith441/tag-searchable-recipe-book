Chipotle & Adobo Shredded Chicken

#mexican #one pot #over an hour

Batch and Time

makes 8 servings
takes 5 hours

Equipment

  • Cutting Board
  • Slow Cooker

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • 3/4 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Broth/Stock
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Adobo Sauce
  • 2 Chipotles in Adobo
  • 2 tsp Cumin
  • Salt & Pepper (vibes based)

For Serving (Optional)

  • 1/2 Lime per person
  • Chips/Tortillas
  • Thinly Sliced Radishes
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Finely chop 4 Cloves of Garlic and 2 Chipotles in Adobo.
  2. Salt and pepper the Chicken then place in a slow cooker.
  3. Pour 3/4 cup Tomato Sauce, 1/4 cup Broth/Stock, 4 Cloves of Garlic, 2 tbsp Adobo Sauce , 2 Chipotles in Adobo, and 2 tsp Cumin over the chicken.
  4. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours (or low for 8 hours). Check it about half way through and add salt if it needs it.
  5. Remove about half the liquid, then shred the chicken with 2 forks, keeping it in the slow cooker.
  6. Serve on chips/tortillas with toppings of your choice.
