Pollo Con Arroz

#spanish #chicken #rice

Batch and Time

makes 4 servings
takes 45 minutes to an hour

Equipment

  • Cutting board
  • Knives
  • Small Pot for Rice
  • Dutch oven

Ingredients

chicken

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ginger
  • Salt & Pepper

Rice

  • 2 Cups Rice
  • 1 Large Tomato
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tbsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Powder
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • Saffron
  • A few handfuls of Feta Cheese
  • 1/2 Lime
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Finely chop 1 Large Onion and 4 Cloves of Garlic and put a pinch of Saffron in a small bowl of water.
  2. Pound and season two chicken breasts with Paprika, Chili Powder, Garlic Powder, Ginger, Salt, and Pepper.
  3. Start cooking 2 Cups of Rice per the rice's instructions.
  4. While the rice is cooking, cook the chicken in a Dutch oven over medium heat until done through.
  5. Remove the chicken from the Dutch oven and add the onion. Cook until the onion is translucent.
  6. Add the chopped garlic, spices (2 tbsp of Turmeric Powder, 2 tbsp of Cumin, 1 tbsp of Paprika, 1 tsp of Cayenne Powder), and tomato paste to the Dutch oven to toast briefly, then add the Saffron water.
  7. Once the rice is done, add it to the Dutch oven, reduce the Dutch oven heat to low, and mix until it is all yellow (If the rice is taking too long, add a little more water to the Dutch oven to stop the spices and allium from burning.)
  8. Slice the chicken, then add it back into the Dutch along with your diced Tomato and a few handfuls of Feta. Squeeze half a lime's worth of juice over the rice and mix in the chicken, tomato, and cheese.
  9. Salt & Pepper to taste then garnish with fresh Parsley.

Notes

If you like avocado, I highly recommend adding some cubed avocado as an additional garnish.
