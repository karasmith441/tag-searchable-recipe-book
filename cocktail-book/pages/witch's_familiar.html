Witch's Familiar

#bourbon

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Bourbon
  • 1/2 oz. Pumpkin Spice Syrup
  • 1/4 oz. Lime Juice
  • 1 Dash Angostura Bitters

Preparation

Stirred over ice.

Notes

Note that this drink is a spin on a Lion's tail, which you can make by just replacing the pumpkin spice syrup with all spice dram and a barspoon of simple.
