Gay Pirate

#carbonated #fizzy #rum #shaken #sparkling

Ingredients

  • 3 oz. Club Soda
  • 3 oz. Spiced Rum
  • 1/2 oz. Lime Juice
  • 1/4 oz. Blackberry Syrup
  • 1/4 oz. Cherry syrup

Preparation

Add Spiced Rum, Lime Juice, and Syrups to a shaker and shake vigorously.
Strain into a tall glass, then top up with Club Soda.
