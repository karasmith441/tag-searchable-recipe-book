Cough Syrup

#gin

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz. Gin
  • 3/4 oz. Amaretto
  • 1/4 oz. Campari

Preparation

Stirred over ice

Notes

It is extremely important to get the measurements exactly right or this cocktail will taste actually taste like its namesake. If it does end up tasting like cough syrup sometimes a dash or two of Angostura Bitters can save it.
